Referral Engine

Your card can become a commission-based sales channel.

1 / Share Every card gets a referral code.

When someone taps the product offer from a customer-owned card, that card owner is attached to the product-page visit.

2 / Attribute The sale keeps the source.

Checkout clicks and intake submissions carry the referral code, owner ID, visitor ID, selected plan, and commission status.

3 / Credit Pay commission only when a card sells.

Start with manual payout review, then upgrade to Stripe Checkout webhooks or Stripe Connect once the channel proves itself.

Customer leads still belong to the customer workspace. Product sale attribution belongs to Ghost AI Solutions so commissions can be tracked without mixing client prospects into Stephen's personal card leads.
